About

The goal of the R/Medicine conference is to promote the use of the R programming environment in the analysis of medical data; showcase novel tools, techniques, and package for analyzing these data; and provide a forum for collaboration within the community.

The conference will cover the use of R in medical fields from Phase I clinical trial design through the use and efficacy of therapies in public use. Topics including drug discover and PK/PD modeling will likely be more appropriate for the R/Pharma conference. Topic areas for R/Medicine include:

  • Reproducible research in medicine
  • Clinical trial design
  • The analysis of clinical trial data
  • Personalized medicine and the analysis of patient records
  • The analysis of genetic data
